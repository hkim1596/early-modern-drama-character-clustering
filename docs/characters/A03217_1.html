Fore God not I. Now afore God, I know not. As I remember me, a tawnie coate. Ten pound in trust vnto Iohn Tawnie-coate. Come fellow Crack, haue you sorted vp those wares? Mark't them with 54. they must be pack't vp. I, and the Poste receiued it two houres since. You are welcome sir, you are welcome. O sir, money customers to vs are best welcome. And we are ready to receiue money: what might we call your name? Your Kin are the more beholding to you, fellow Cracke turne o're the Callender, and looke for Iohn Good-fellow. You will haue no more wares with you, will you sir. I thinke thou art mop-ey'd this morning, giue me the booke, Letter I, Letter I, Letter I: when had you your ware? Your name's Iohn Good-fellow you say, Letter I, Letter I, Letter I: You doe not come to mocke vs, doe you. Letter I, Let ter I, Letter I. By this hand if I thought you did, I would knocke you about the eares afore wee parted fellow Cracke, get mee a Cudgell ready; Letter I, Letter I, Letter I: Foote here's no such name in all our Booke. Doe you heare fellow are you drunke this morning, to make vs looke for moone-shine in the water? And I tell thee Iohn Goodfellow, here's no such name in our booke, nor no such ware deliuered. Doe you heare Hoyden, and my master were not in the next roome, Ide knocke you about eares for playing the knaue with vs, ere you parted. Fut, here's a mad slaue indeed will giue vs ten pound in spight of our teethes. I hope we know sir you doe not vse to bring vp your ser uants to receiue money vnlesse it be due vnto you. You are an early riser, my good Lord, You haue said true, we are starters in one houre, And our attendance is to waight one such a Queene, VVhose vertue all the world: but to leaue that VVhich euery tongue is glad to commune with, Since Monsiers first arriuall in the land, The time that he was here, and the time since, VVhat royaltie hath beene in Englands Court, Both princely reuelling, and war-like sport. M. Doctor Parry. This Summer morning makes vs couetous To take the profit of the pleasant ayre. Fie M. Doctor, Your face beares not the habite it was wont, And your discourse is altered, what's the matter? Men beeing as you are, so great in grace With such a royall Princesse, haue no reason. M. Gresham, you are well met. I but M. Gresham, a man may beare till his backe breake. We should be glad to see it. But I dare sware she is no suter to you. To Morrow shall we attend your worship. Fiftie pound, Madame.
