Mort diew. O Ver bon: excellenta gull, he tak'a my Lord Chamoun for Mounſieur Gaſpra, & Mounſieur Gaſpra for my Lord Cha mont, ô dis be braue for make a me laugh'a, ha, ha, ha, ô my heart tickla. Vtteria? vat Mounſieur? O pardone moy, fore my tongue ſhall put out de ſecreta, Shall breede de cankra in my mouth. Foe, you ſhall not heare foole, for all your long eare, Reguard Mounſieur: you be de Chamont, Chamont be Gaſ pra. Contenta: Reguarde, vou le Preimer. Mort deiu le peſant. Mounſieur Onion, vene ca, come hidera, Ie vou prey. By gar me ha ſee two, tree, foure hundra towſand of your Couſan hang. Lend me your hand, ſhall prey for know you bettra. A we fort boon: ſtand? you be deere now, me come, Boon iour Mounſieur. By gar, be muſh glad for ſee you. Nay, ſhall make you ſay rare by and by, Reguard Mounſieur Finio, Dieu vou gard Mounſieur. Mounſieur On ion? is not fort boon. O here, Void de grand admiration, as ſhould meet per chance Mounſieur Finio, Ieſu? by Gar who thinke wee ſhall meete here? Well what ſay you for dis den? Mounſieur: Par ma foy vou bein encounters? Comen, ſe porte la ſante. Trot, be muſh ioy for heire. Fat comme vou voyer. Que que, parla Mounſeir, what iſt. O me ſhall doe preſently, ſtand you deere, you ſigni or deere, my ſelfe is here: ſo fort bein, now I parle to Moun ſeir Onion, Onion pratla to you, you ſpeaka to me, ſo, and as you parle chang the bonet, Mounſeir Onion. Pray be couera. Pardon moy, ſhall be ſo, By gar you muſt. You be big enough for de Onion already, By gar me ſhall be hang for tella dis ſame, Metella madamoy ſelle, ſhe tell her ſadera. O pardone moy mounſieur, O Mounſieur, no point villaine, braue Cheualier, Mounſieur Gaſper. Me canno tell, par ma foy Mounſieur. Aue mounſieur. Giue me de name, vat name: What? turne vpon the toe. By gar, ſhal not alone follow you, but ſhal leade you to. Fat ſo me tinke.
