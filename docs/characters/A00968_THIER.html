You are here in a sanctuary; and that viper (Who since he hath forgot to be a sonne, I much disdaine to thinke of as a brother) Had better in despight of all the gods, To haue razed their Temples; and spurn'd downe their altars, Then in his impious abuse of you, To haue called on my iust anger. Believing that Theodoret was not his brother and that Ordella is dead, Thierry offers to make amends by marrying Memberge, Theodoret's daughter. Brunhild is forced to admit her lies in order to prevent an incestuous marriage. She gives Thierry a poisoned handkerchief, which kills him; Martel brings Ordella to his deathbed. Brunhild admits complicity in his murder, and is sentenced to remain awake for the rest of her life; disturbed by the sight of Protaldi being tortured to death, she commits suicide. Thierry and Ordella die together , and Martel becomes King of France.
