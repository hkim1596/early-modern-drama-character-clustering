Hum. Hum If my Taylor had not bin a Knaue— But— What ayle you, Lady? Me thinkes sirrah, since my tongue brake loose, I take an infinite pleasure in't, how thinkes thou? It shall run before any mans bond for a wager. My honourable Lord, how does the great Dutchesse, Your sister? With the rest of your kindred and acquaintance that bore the Court before 'hem. And mangie ones to, are we not? Ha, ha, prithee laugh at him. Hang him, hee dares not within the confines of the Court. Why, come then lets goe backe againe? For my owne part, I donnot care for quarrelling there wee may be bold, though my Lord, I haue a poore sister I wud prefer to cut your Honors toes. I donnot like this. It did me more good then my dinner, I protest, to see her transported to prison againe. The Duke accepts that his disembodied voice was sent by providence to exonerate an innocent woman, and Dorigen is freed.
