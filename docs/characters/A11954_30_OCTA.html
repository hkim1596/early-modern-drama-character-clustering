Your Brother too muſt dye: conſent you Lepidus ? Pricke him downe Antony. Or heere, or at the Capitoll. So you thought him, And tooke his voyce who ſhould be prickt to dye In our blacke Sentence and Proſcription. You may do your will: But hee's a tried, and valiant Souldier. Let vs do ſo: for we are at the ſtake, And bayed about with many Enemies, And ſome that ſmile haue in their hearts I feare Millions of Miſcheefes. Now Antony, our hopes are anſwered, You ſaid the Enemy would not come downe, But keepe the Hilles and vpper Regions: It proues not ſo: their battailes are at hand, They meane to warne vs at Philippi heere: Anſwering before we do demand of them. Vpon the right hand I, keepe thou the left. I do not croſſe you: but I will do ſo. Mark Antony, ſhall we giue ſigne of Battaile? No Caeſar, we will anſwer on their Charge. Make forth, the Generals would haue ſome words. Not that we loue words better, as you do. Come, come, the cauſe. If arguing make vs ſwet, The proofe of it will turne to redder drops: Looke, I draw a Sword againſt Conſpirators, When thinke you that the Sword goes vp againe? Neuer till Caeſars three and thirtie wounds Be well aueng'd; or till another Caeſar Haue added ſlaughter to the Sword of Traitors. So I hope: I was not borne to dye on Brutus Sword. Come Antony : away: Defiance Traitors, hurle we in your teeth. If you dare fight to day, come to the Field; If not, when you haue ſtomackes. What man is that? All that ſeru'd Brutus, I will entertaine them. Fellow, wilt thou beſtow thy time with me? Do ſo, good Meſſala. According to his Vertue, let vs vſe him Withall Reſpect, and Rites of Buriall.
