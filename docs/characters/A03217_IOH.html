Your Worship must haue such as the Countrey yeelds, or none at all. But I pray ye sir, what's our trade? And that the worst trade in all Christendome, and espe cially for French women: If they know a man to be a Haberda sher of small ware, thee'le haue no dealing with him, and there fore and you will haue any good Commodities here, you must change your copie, you neuer were a traueller: and therefore you know not what belongs too't; but you doe cleane mistake this Gentlewoman, and you take her for a light wench, weigh her in equall ballance, and you shall find her no such woman, no such woman Ile assure you. Fore God sir, I would not haue you wrong the Gentle womans repute, for a world. This Metressa deales for herselfe and hath many sorts of ware at commaund, I was now bargai ning with her about a certaine Countrey Commoditie, and had not your co ̄ ming mar'd the Match, one had gone through for't. And further should you wrong the Ladies reputation heere in Fraunce, Ile assure you they haue the law of their sides, but to confirme your good opinion of her, this is she of whom I tooke vp your Commoditie of Matches; be sorry for your offence, and excuse you to her for shame maister. Nor she of English, but al's one; vpon her Master, and what you cannot doe in words, perfourme in dumbe signes: What in your slippers come to take me napping!
