O brother heeres a change, Since old Sir Charles died in our fathers house Sir, we feed sparing and we labor hard We lie vneasie, to reserue to vs. And our succession this small plot of ground. My harts so hardned with the frost of griefe, Death connot pierce it through, Tyrant too fel, So lead the Fiends condemned soules to hel. Acton that seekes our blood. Run away. I was not borne a begger, though his extreames Enforce this language from me, I protest No fortune of mine could lead my tongue To this base key. I do beseech you vncle, For the names sake, for Christianity, Nay for Gods sake to pitty his distresse: He is denied the freedome of the prison, And in the hole is laid with men condemnd, Plenty he ha h of nothing but of yrons, And it remaines in you to free him thence. Gold is but earth: thou earth inough shalt haue When thou hast once tooke measure of thy graue: You know me maister Sandy and my sute. Cold comfort stil: what say you chosen Tydy ? Oh charity why art thou fled to heauen, And left al things on this earth vneuen, Their scosfing answeres I wll nere returne, But to my selfe his griefe in silence mourne. I thanke you Heauens, I thanke you gentle sir? God make me able to requite this fauor. Acton : oh God that name I am borne to cursse, Hence Bawd: hence Broker: see, I spurne his gold, My honor neuer shal for gaine be sold. From you Ile posting hie, Euen as the Doues from feathered Eagles flie. I can no longer stay from visiting My woful brother, while I could I kept My haples tidings tom his hopeful eare. What, at liberty? Charles, can you mocke me in your pouerty. Brother, why haue you trict me like a bride? Bought me this gay attire, these ornaments? forget you our estate, our pouerty? Oh brother, what doth this strange language meane? By me: why? I haue nothing, nothing, left, I owe euen for the clothes vpon my backe, I am not worth, &c. Till now I had thought you loud me, by mine honor Which I had kept as spotlesse as the Moone, I nere was mistris of that single doite, Which I reserud not to supply your wants: And do you think that I would hoord from you.
