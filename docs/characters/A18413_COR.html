O, my Lord Medice became it rarely, Me thought I likde his manlie being out; It becomes Noblemen to do … nothing well. Madam, I thinke you'le see a prettie shew. See here, my Lord, I haue rob'd all their Caskets, Know you this Ring? this Carquanet? this Chaine? Will any of these letters serue your turne? Come then, come follow me; I am perswaded I shall go neare to shew you their kind hands. Their confidence, that you are still a hunting, Will make your amorous sonne that stole from thence, Bold in his loue-sports; Come, come, a fresh chace, I hold this pickelocke, you shall hunt at view. What, do they thinke to seape? An old wiues eye Is a blew Cristall full of sorcerie.
