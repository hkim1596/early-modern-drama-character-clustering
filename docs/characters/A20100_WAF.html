To morrow weele send him to thee: til then sweet Tenter hook we leaue thee, wishing thou maist haue the fortune to cha ̄ ge thy name often. I, for theeues and widdowes loue to shift many names, and make sweet vse of it to. Prethee go in and empty them, come be not so impatient. You wil not go to night, I am sure.
