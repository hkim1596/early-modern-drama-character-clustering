Plot summary

Plot — The Masque of Beauty
Boreas reminds January of the twelve Ethiopian women who were to return to court the following year after being made beautiful: they have twice failed to do so. Boreas explains that this was not through ingratitude: four of their sisters, also hoping to be beautified, have been intercepted by the envious Night and kept prisoner on a floating island, and the whitened women have been delayed in seeking their freedom. Vulturnus arrives with the news that the prisoners are freed, the women have taken command of the island, and are using it to travel to England. They arrive and are welcomed by the Thames. Singing and dancing ensues. The island ceases to float and comes to rest permanently in Britain.
