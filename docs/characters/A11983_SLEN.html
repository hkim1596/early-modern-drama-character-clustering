Nay surely my vncle shall not put it vp so. I haue matter in my head against you and your cogging companions, Pistoll and Nym. They carried mee to the Tauerne and made mee drunke, and afterward picked my pocket. I by this handkercher did he. Two faire shouell boord shillings, besides seuen groats in mill sixpences. By this light it was he then. Nay for my owne part, I would litle or no thing with you. I loue you well, and my vncle can tell you how my liuing stands. And if you can loue me why so.
