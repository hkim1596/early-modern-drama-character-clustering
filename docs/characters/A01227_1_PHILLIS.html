When that I seeme to repent, or seeme to bewray my repentance, Or say so with teares; let floods run back to the fountaines, Let Woolues fly fro the Lambe, and trembling Hare to the Greyhounde, Let Beares range by the sea, and Dolphin swymme by the deserte.
