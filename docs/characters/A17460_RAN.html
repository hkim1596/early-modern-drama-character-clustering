And you sweet Seignior—'Tis very precious hot, I protest I haue bin cooler vnder the Line. I wud we might command some Drinke here. This Lord has a most dulcid situation, pleasant, and profitable, I haue seldome seene in Venice a sweeter. I cannot tell indeed, I did not marke 'hem. I cannot tell, I neuer had much conuersation with your people of Hybla, my noble Lord. Your Lordships Iennet in my conceit is a most vnderstanding Beast. I saw the Adlantatho of Domingo mounted vpon such another, not much different, nay surely nothing at all; and I donnot thinke but they were twinnes, onely I thinke your Lordships the better. Your Lordship keepes the Saddle admirable, I wud your Lordship had bin with me at Iapon, I speake it for Horses sake, and H … semanship toge ther; I protest they are the best Ryders, if I said the Rankest, I did not lye: for beleeue it, I hold your Ranke Ryder a good Ryder, take him alwayes, and at all times: As your Frenchman, in Christendome I donnot know a ranker Ryder, vnlesse it be some part North, there they are Scourers. Your Lordship has a most neat Ruffe, and becomes you most elegantly. Will your Lordship conduct? Faire gentle Milke maid. Good; a Wench of Language: by this hand I loue thee. But to weake vnderstandings now I come, Is your small Taber musicke to your Drum. Or in instrument of peace, can there that triall Be made vpon a Kit as a base Viole. Iudge you my Masters, that on both haue playd, It is but my opinion, and I haue sayd.
