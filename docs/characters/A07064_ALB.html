Oh, a horned diuell would prooue ominous, Vnto a bridegroomes eyes, Dead? Sweet Prince, be patient. Tis reasons glorie to commaund affects. Take comfort He was the very hope of Italy, Musick sounds softly. The blooming honour of your drooping age. Vncle, this laughter ill becomes your griefe. NAY sweet be comforted, take counsell and My Lord, we dare not leaue you thus alone. Will you endeauour to forget your griefe? Since you enforce vs, faire prince, we are gone. O me most miserable. Fie, tis vnsuting to your elate spirite: Rather put on some transshap't caualier, Some habit of a spitting Critick, whose mouth Voids nothing but gentile and vnuulgar Rheume of censure: rather assume ▪ You can not presse among the courtiers, And haue accesse to O black accursed fate. Antonyo's drown'd. As I doe loue Piero, he is drownde. Distraught and rauing, from a turrets top He threwe his bodie in the high swolne sea, And as he headlong topsie turuie dingd downe, He still cri'd Mellida. The Duke drinkes deepe to ouerdowe his griefe. The court is rackt to pleasure, each man straines To faine a iocund eye. The Florentine Euen he is mightie on our part.
