Your purpose Sir, is to loue this Lady, And hazard all your hopes. But for your loue. Then 'twere more dishonorable To strumpet her. Braue Prince I'me glad That ere I kept thy Company. Faith my Lord, I neuer loue a woman for her habite, When sir I loue, I'le fee my lone starke naked.
