No, to giue thee Some good security, and see Pecunia. Wee'l be one bound for another. This worthy Courtier. But a Sea-Captaine now, braue Captaine Shun-field. He holds vp his nose. And season'd too, since he tooke salt at Sea. Here's a Gentleman, A Fresh-man i'the world, one Master Madrigall. And full hopes: A dainty Scholler, and a pretty Poët! Come, thou lou'st to be costiue Still i' thy curt'sie; but I haue a pill, A golden pill to purge away this melancholly. Some dosser of Fish Or Fowle to fetch of. Not the diuell. O! the onely Oracle That euer peept, or spake out of a dublet. A sodden head, and his whole braine a possit curd! I wonder what religion hee's of! Hang thee dog. Well wolfe, Hyaena, you old pockie rascall, You will ha' the Hernia fall downe againe Into your Scrotum, and I shall be sent for. I will remember then, that; and your Fistula In ano, I cur'd you of. What an vngratefull wretch is this? He may doe some good With him perhaps. Where ha' you beene Madrigall? Sir, by both Your worshipfull Titles, and your name Mas Broker. Good morrow. A creature of two natures, Because hee has two Offices. But this is a hungry soile, And must be helpt. Our sweete Songster Shall rarifie't into ayre. Sweepes downe no cobwebs here, But sells 'hem for cut-fingers. And the spiders, As creatures rear'd of dust, and cost him nothing, To fat old Ladies monkeyes. Where's my fellow Fitton? With the Muses? They' were euer three in Poetry. We may haue a ieere, perhaps. Who? The glory o'the Kitchin? that holds Cookery, A trade from Adam? quotes his broths ▪ and sallads? And sweares he's not dead yet, but translated In some immortall crust, the past of Almonds? I was at an Olla Podrida of his making, Was a braue piece of cookery! at a funerall! But opening the pot-lid, he made vs laugh, who'had wept all day! and sent vs such a tickling Into our nostrills, as the funerall feast Had bin a wedding-dinner. I, and Pseudodoxes. Mary, for most, he's Orthodox i'the Kitchin. I, and the Layties ! We first begin with our selues, & then at you, I wonder all his Clients were not there. 'Tis indeed, Sir. When we doe speake at volley, all the ill We can one of another. That Mony-bawd. A very Rascall! A supercilious Rogue! he lookes as if He were the Patrico — The Law, And he does gouerne him But what gingling Gipsies, And Pedlers trade in— This fury shewes, if there were nothing else! And 'tis diuine! I shall for euer hereafter, Admire the wisedome of a Cooke! The Floures did nurse! They all be ginne the ncomium of Pecunia. Her eyes their lights, and riualls to the Starres! And polish'd skinne, whiter then Venus foote! A haire, Large as the Mornings, and her breath as sweete, As meddowes after raine, and but new mowne! Hermione for brests! And Helen for a mouth! But loseth by it: here the Snow thawes Snow; One frost resolues another! And glances that beguile the seers eyes! I cannot thinke he finisheth that.
