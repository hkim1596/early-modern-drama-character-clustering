For our Antonius in the heat of all His active life knew how to revell well. No Marcus no; alas theſe petty Kingdomes (Though too too great to be ſo ill beſtow'd) Are not the ſcope of her ambitious aymes! How amorous in his language he is grown. Cyprus, Phoenice, Coeloſyria, Three wealthy Kingdoms got with Roman bloud, And our forefathers valour, given away As the baſe hire of an adulterous bed.
