That day is sure a notable informer; yet I believe He spyes more bad then he mends. You say well neighbour: And a st ale hare with a great pudding in her belly. Yes neighbour, and a choller of Brawne that was fatten'd with stale porredge. No neighbour, let the young couple goe, and much joy go with them. Let us take up our rests in this thic ket, or the next house; for I am as sleepy as if I had eaten a Puppie. Yes, a Puppie; I heard our Landlords Carter speake it last Whitsontide in a Play. Why then resolv'd: 'twill be day presently: let's put ont the candle, and go to bed, and farewel Landlord. Exeunt. And the Divell your Executor.
