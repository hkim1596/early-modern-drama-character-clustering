Plot — 478
Eteocles and Polynices are elected co-kings of Thebes, ruling in alternate years. At the end of the first year, Eteocles refuses to stand down and banishes Polynices. Six champions join Polynices to besiege Thebes, but, after heavy losses, Polynices offers to settle the case by single combat. He and Eteocles kill one another, and Creon takes the crown.
