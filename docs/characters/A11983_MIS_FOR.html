How now Mistris Page, are you reading Loue Letters? How do you woman? Ile match your letter iust with the like, Line for line, word for word. Only the name Of misteris Page, and misteris Foord disagrees: Do me the kindnes to looke vpon this. Reuenged, if we liue weel be reuenged. O Lord if my husband should see this Letter, I faith this would euen giue edge to his Iealousie. Well I thanke you good M. Page. How now husband, how chaunce thou art so me lancholy? God saue me, see who yonder is: Weele set her a worke in this businesse. I follow you Mistresse Page. Sirrha, if your M. aske you whither You carry this basket, say to the Launderers, I hope you know how to bestow it? Go get you in. Well sir Iohn, I beleeue I shall serue you such a trick, You shall haue little mind to come againe. Why how then sir Iohn? Alas sir Iohn, I should be a verie simple Ladie. But I hope tis not true Misteris Page. Alas mistresse Page, what shall I do? Here is a gentleman my friend, how shall I do? Alas I feare he is too big.
