Lictors , away. Sir, His banish'd into Pontia , by the Senate. DRVSVS is prisoner in the palace. The princesse is confin'd, to Pandataria. Treason. True, and to day, one of SEIANVS friends Honour'd by speciall writ; and on the morrow Another punish'd— These forked tricks, I vnderstand 'hem not, Would he would tell vs whom he loues, or hates, That we might follow, without feare, or doubt. But there are letters come (they say) eu'n now, Which doe forbid that last. Yes. But is that true, it 'tis prohibited, To sacrifice vnto him? Is it no other? He names him here without his titles. No other, then SEIANVS. Indeed? Sir, both come well. Seuen cohorts. I cannot the iust number: but, I thinke, Three centuries. At most, not foure. That the Consul Can best deliuer you. I, and I am— They are more— My life, And all I hold— Yes. I vnderstand so much. But how it can— The keeping of the watch in armes, When morning comes. I, and to name a man! He, that would nor be lords foole, nor the worlds. MACRO! most welcome, as most coueted friend! Let me enioy my longings. When arriu'd you? Wilt please your lordship to command me— I. He is wise, will make him friends Of such, who neuer loue, but for their ends.
