Vnciuillie, mew, can a Lord talke vnciuilly? I thinke you a finicall taffatae pipkin may be proud ile sit so neare it, vnciuillie mew. Prithee but note yo Felow, do's he not walke & look as if hee did desire to be a Cuckold? How dost thou Coze, hast thou got any more wit yet? Good Maister Wel-tri'd, you can informe mee, pray how ended the quarrell betwixt yong Bould, and the other Gentleman. T'is strange there was no hurt done, yet I hold the other Gentleman, farr the better Man. Besides they say the satisfaction that walks in the Or dinaries, is counter-feit. What d'ee this afternoone. Nay afaith let's vp and haue a rest at Primero. Farewell, and lets haue no excuses, pray. My Horse there, Z'oones I would not for the world He should alight before me in the field, My name and henor were for euer lost. Why, how now good man flat-cap, what d'ee lack? Who doe you talke to, sirrha? Arrest me, rogue? I am a Lord ye curs, a Parliament man. At whose suit? Why thou base rogue, did not I set thee vp, Hauing no stock, but thy faire shop and wise? A plot, a trick by Heauen. See Ingens foote-boy, t'is by his Masters meanes, oh coward, slaue; i'le put in baile, or pay the debt. Ingen, thou art a worthy Gentleman, for this curtesie, Go-too i'le saue thy life, come on Sir: hay, I'le cut your codpeice point Sir, with this thrust, And then downe goes your breeches. S'hart, t'is a louing rogue. Hence away it is a Sisters losse, that whets my sword. Oh, haue at you Sir. Sai'st true sister. Mine own best Sister, farewel M r . Ingen. I and well, you pronounce rarely did you neuer play? Looke to my sister. How fares she M r . Doctor. Z'oons, whats here Bould, Widdow, Wel-tri'd, Fee-simple, hoy-day.
