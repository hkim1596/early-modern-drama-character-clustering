Gods lid man, ſeruice is ready to go vp man, you muſt ſlip on your coate and come in, we lacke waiters pitty fully. Well youle come. I would not you ſhould thinke ſo ſir, for though I haue no learning, yet I honour a ſcholer in any ground of the earth ſir, Shall I requeſt your name ſir? O did my fellow Iuniper tell you? marry ſir, ſhe is as one may ſay, but a poore mans child indeede, and for mine owne part I am no Gentleman borne I muſt confeſſe, but my mind to me a kingdome is truly. T'is ſomewhat ſtale, but that's no matter. This is but a hungry compariſon in my iudgement. No faith ſir, but there goes a huge report on't. Tut then I ſhall not chuſe but like it. You are in the right, I'le not giue a halfepeny to ſee a thouſand on'hem. I was at one the laſt Tearme, but & euer I ſee a more roguiſh thing, I am a peece of cheeſe, & no onion, nothing but kings & princes in it, the foole came not out a lot. No, it were a vaine thing, and you ſhould ſir. Indeed that's right, you are in print already for the beſt plotter. How now friend, what are you there? be vncouered, Would you ſpeake with any man here? Do you acknowledge it? nay then you ſhall go forth, Ile teach you how ſhall acknowledge it another time; go to, void, I muſt haue the hall purg'd, no ſetting vp of a reſt here, packe, begone. Gods ſo, Valentine! Mary, to morrow ſhall be three months, ſhe was ſeene going to heauen they ſay, about ſome fiue weekes agone! how now? trickling teares, ha?
