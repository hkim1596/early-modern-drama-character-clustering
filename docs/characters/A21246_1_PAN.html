He identifies the Queen as the woman of the Satyrs’ dreams, greets her, and joins her train. Further down the hill, Pan attempts to woo two shepherdesses. They reject him and ask him to give their mother warning of the Queen’s approach, but he is rooted to the spot with wonder. He submits to the Queen and wishes her a happy visit.
