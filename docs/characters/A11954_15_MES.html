From France to England, neuer ſuch a powre For any forraigne preparation, Was leuied in the body of a land. The Copie of your ſpeede is learn'd by them: For when you ſhould be told they do prepare, The tydings comes, that they are all arriu'd. My Liege, her eare Is ſtopt with duſt: the firſt of Aprill di'de Your noble mother; and as I heare, my Lord, The Lady Conſtance in a frenzie di'de Three dayes before: but this from Rumors tongue I idely heard: if true, or falſe I know not. Vnder the Dolphin. With all my heart, my Liege. My Lord: your valiant kinſman Falconbridge, Deſires your Maieſtie to leaue the field, And ſend him word by me, which way you go. Be of good comfort: for the great ſupply, That was expected by the Dolphin heere, Are wrack'd three nights ago on Goodwin ſands. This newes was brought to Richard but euen now, The French fight coldly, and retyre themſelues. Where is my Prince, the Dolphin? The Count Meloone is ſlaine: The Engliſh Lords By his perſwaſion, are againe falne off, And your ſupply, which you haue wiſh'd ſo long, Are caſt away, and ſunke on Goodwin ſands. Who euer ſpoke it, it is true my Lord.
