Heare vs great kings, vouchſafe awhile to ſtay And I ſhall ſhew you peace, and faire-fac'd league: Win you this Citie without ſtroke, or wound, Reſcue thoſe breathing liues to dye in beds, That heere come ſacrifices for the field. Perſeuer not, but heare me mighty kings. That daughter there of Spaine, the Lady Blanch Is neere to England, looke vpon the yeeres Of Lewes the Dolphin, and that louely maid. If luſtie loue ſhould go in queſt of beautie, Where ſhould he finde it fairer, the in Blanch: If zealous loue ſhould go in ſearch of vertue, Where ſhould he finde i purer then in Blanch? If loue ambitious, ſought a match of birth, Whoſe veines bound richer blood then Lady Blanch?
