Shepheard how camst thou hether to this place? No way is troden, all the verdent grasse The spring shot vp stands yet vnbrused heere Of any foote, onely the dappld deere: Farre from the feared sound of crooked horne Dwels in this fastnesse. If any art I haue, or hidden skill, May cure thee of disease or festred ill, Whose griefe or greenenesse to anothers eie, May seeme vnpossible of remedie, I dare yet vndertake it. Then shepheard let me sue To knowe thy griefe that man yet neuer knew The way to health, that durst not shew his sore. Swaine no more.
