What Sir? will you take vp the best chamber, and spend but two pence for your part. And this at Croyden so neere London? There is no such stuffe now in the cloake-bagge I trow? If I thought there were, I would throw it to the dogs rather then it should ride behinde me. That shalbe tried by and by. Be these his trickes? though I be his horse-keeper, Ile carrie no coales nor bones neither I trow. And further, I had like to catch'd her. All this companie can tell what a course I had at her, if you will not beleeue me. I were a foole indeed, and a beast too, if I would put them vp.
