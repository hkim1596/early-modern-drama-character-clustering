Now Captaine Obſeruation, times bawde, thou that haſt kept the Ages doore, whilſt vp-ſtart baſenes crept into the bedde of greatneſſe, what dooſt thou thinke of this change? Thats no part of my meaning, yet would I gladly be bet ter inſtructed why the Duke broke vp his Court in Archadea, and remoued it into this Iland? Your words throw ſence into mee, and thats the cauſe the Iland is ſo ſurely guarded with watch-towers, ouer which our ſelu and other Captaines haue the charge. If inquiſitiueneſſe be not too bolde a gueſt, what doe you thinke of Dametas. Your compariſon holds, for by report, his auarice has vn made many to make him vp. As ſome doe vpon offices, by fortune and flatterie, or as truth ſaies, the Prince hauing one day loſt his way, wandring in the woods found this Dametas, affected his diſcourſe, tooke him along to the Court, and like great men in loue with their owne dooings, countenanct his defects, gaue him offices, titles, and all the additions that g e to the making vp of a man worſhipfull. Your thoughts and mine are twynnes in that: but I heare the warning bell, ſome ſtrangers are ariued. A couple of peticioners, ant like your worſhip. That fellowes pocket is like a Taylers hell, it eates vp part of euery mans due: tis an Executioner, and makes away more inno cent petitions in one yeere, then a red-headed hang man cuts ropes in an age. The next way to make it the Ile of fooles. Cry your worſhip mercy, I had forgot your authoritie. Cry your authoritie mercy, will you diſcharge vs of theſe paſſengers? Bad fate, that wrong ſhould ſet his foote on right, And true borne Eagles ſtoope to this baſe kyte. No matter for reaſon theirs rime enough and that be good. Sure he had ſome reaſon to make this rime, and a man could pick it out, But wheres the wag that inuited vs to this banquet of mirth ſhrunke in the wetting? that or ſome knauery elſe vpon my life, I had the boy in ſhrowd ſuſpiſion at the firſt.
