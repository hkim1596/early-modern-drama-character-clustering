You meane a foole sir. Then I apply it fit: the one shee takes of her father, the other of her mother, now you may bee sure she is your owne. There is verie good workmanship in it, but the matter is but base, if the stuffe had bene as good as the mold, your daughter had bene as wise as she is beautifull I, & so cunningly, that she toke it not fro ̄ you. A good euidence to proue the fee-simple of your daughters folly. It came by nature, and if none can take it a waie, it is perpetuall. Well, what followes? Why with a payre of compasses, and bring them both into the circle, He warrant the'il match themselues. O then, so it be a match you care not. A verie good nature. Well, if by flat wit I bring this to passe, whats my rewerd? Ile aske no more than by my wit I can get in the bargaine. If I come not about you neuer trust mee, Ile seeke out Dromio, the counseller of my conceit. Oh that it were my chance Obuiam dare Dro mio, to stumble vpon Dromio, on whome I doo no thing but dreame. He to cosin, & I to coniure, would make such alterations, that our masters shuld serue themselues, the ideots their children serue vs, and we to wake our wits betweene them all. My master olde Stellio hath a foole to his daughter. I must conuey a controct. Betweene her and Memphios sonne, with out speaking one to another. Doest thou mocke me Dromio? Not I for all this is true. Then are we both driuen to our wits endes, for if either of them had bin wise, wee might haue tempered, if no marriage, yet a close marriage. Dromio looke heere, now is my hand on my halfepenie. Be not angrie Halfepenie, for fellowship we will be all fooles, and for gaine all knaues. But why doest thou laugh? Whats the matter? Thou art a foole, that cannot be. Prethee how? Go to. Nay the bodkin is heere alreadie, you must be the knife. Thine will be the worst, I feare we shall leaue a halfepenie in hand. If you be examined how we met, sweare by chance, for so they met, and therefore will beleeue it: if how much we drunke, let them answere them selues, they know best because they paid it.
