Oh god my hart: A prisoner in the Tower, Speake to the Queene my Lords, that some other place May lodge her sister, that's too vild, too base. Then to our chamber comfortlesse and sad, To morrow to the tower that fatall place, Where I shall neuer behold the sunnes bright face.
