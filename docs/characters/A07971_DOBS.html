But I would I had stay'd still in the Countrey, now Sports are tollerated, in despite of Justice, Trouble some's malicious Authoritie. I had rather see a Morris dance and a May-pole, then ten Playes: what care I for wit which I understand not? How, Asse to my face! provoke me no more with such foule language, lest I enter and act thy Tragedy. Nay, my Master is as tall a man of his inches.
