No my good Lord, they bee men of no great account, For they bee none but Tylers, Thatchers, Millers, and ſuch like. That in their liues did neuer come in field, Before this mutanie did call them ſorth: And for ſecuritie of my backe returne, Vpon this meſſage which I ſhowd the King, They keepe my wife and children for a pledge, And hald mee out from forth my Caſtell at Rocheſter, And ſwore me there to come vnto your Maieſtie, And hauing told you their mindes, I hope your Grace will pardon mee for all: In that I am enforced therevnto. I thinke my Lord about twentie thouſand men, But if your Grace would follow my aduice, Thus would I deale with theſe Rebellious men, I would finde time to parly with ſome of them, And know what in their mindes they doo intend, For being armed with ſuch treacherous thoughts, They may performe more than your Grace expects.
