In the meane time, what heare you of theſe Warres? Nay, I aſſure you a peace concluded. I perceiue by this demand, you are not alto gether of his councell. Sir, his wife ſome two months ſince fledde from his houſe, her pretence is a pilgrimage to Saint Ia ques le grand ; which holy vndertaking, with moſt au ſtere ſanctimonie ſhe accompliſht: and there reſiding, the tenderneſſe of her Nature, became as a prey to her greefe: in fine, made a groane of her laſt breath, & now ſhe ſings in heauen.
