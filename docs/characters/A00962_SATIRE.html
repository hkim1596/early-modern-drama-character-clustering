See fayre Goddesse in the wood, They haue let out yet more blood: Some sauadge man hath strucke her brest So soft and white, that no wild beast, Durst a toucht asleepe or wake, So sweete that Adder, Neut, or Snake. Would haue layn from arme to arme, On her Bossome to be warme, All a night and being hot, Gone away and stung her not.
