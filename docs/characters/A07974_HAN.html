And you are pleas'd with them? That can Hanniball; Who through the twarthy vizard age and cares Have tann'd his face with, blusheth at the change Of Souldiers into women. Why in stead Of Plume-crown'd crests weare you not tyres? and deck Your neckes with gemmes, in stead of arming them With Corslets?
